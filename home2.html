Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our 24/7 support concierge for elite assistance.

Contact Concierge
What vetting process do your Elite Walkers undergo?
Every elite walker undergoes a multi-layer process including a federal background check, a pet psychology assessment, and a mandatory 40-hour field residency with a senior co-op mentor.
Can I request the same walker for every session?
Consistency is the cornerstone of our service. Our Elite members are assigned a primary and secondary walker to ensure your pet builds a deep, lasting bond with a familiar face.
How does the GPS real-time tracking work?
Through our proprietary member dashboard, you can follow your pet's precise path, view heart-rate data (if wearing a compatible tracker), and receive instant "paws-on-the-ground" notifications.
Is my home insured during pet sitting?
Every visit is protected by our $5M comprehensive liability policy, which covers both your pets and your property, providing absolute peace of mind while you are away.
