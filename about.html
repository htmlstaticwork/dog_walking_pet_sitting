Community-Driven Care Since 2024

Petly was founded on a simple principle: those who care for our pets should be treated with the same respect as our pets themselves.

A New Model of Trust

Unlike traditional gig-economy apps, Petly is owned and operated by the people who perform the care. When you book with us, you aren't just getting a service; you are supporting a local professional who has real stake in their community.

This means lower turnover, better knowledge of local parks, and a deeper bond with your furry family members.

How We Ensure Safety

Background Checks

Every walker undergoes rigorous local and national background screenings.

Mandatory Training

First-aid and behavioral training are requirements for all our members.

Full Insurance

We provide industry-leading insurance for every single visit and walk.

Community Reviews

Real feedback from real neighbors keeps our standards high.
